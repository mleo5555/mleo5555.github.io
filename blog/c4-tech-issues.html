Online Security

8/3/15

The internet is being integrated into our lives more ways than we can count in 2015. The average American can access the internet from the palm of their hand with a smartphone, wirelessly from almost any location. With more people using the internet for things like transferring money and exchanging private information, hackers are on the rise. Identity theft and stolen credit card information are just a couple examples of the types of online theft that occur every single day. Criminals no longer need to leave their house to steal money from strangers due to the gift of the internet.

With a rise in internet hackers brings a rise in the push to improve internet security. Keeping users secure is big business in the tech industry and the demand for quality protection is growing.

Tips to Stay Protected

  • Use a long and unique password
  • Always update your software whenever updates become available
  • Never download anything unless you know the website is safe
